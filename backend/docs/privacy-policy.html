Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Apple ID: When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique identifier and optionally your email address.
  • Video Prompts: The text prompts you submit for video generation are temporarily stored and sent to OpenAI's API.
  • Usage Data: We track credit usage, video generation history, and API requests for billing and service quality purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your account
  • Process video generation requests via OpenAI's Sora API
  • Manage credit balances and purchases
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Sharing

We share data only when necessary:

  • OpenAI: Your video prompts are sent to OpenAI for processing
  • Apple: We use Sign in with Apple for authentication and in-app purchases

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Retention

  • Videos: Download URLs expire after 24 hours and are not stored by us
  • Prompts: Stored for 30 days for debugging, then deleted
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active

5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure database storage on Cloudflare infrastructure, and regular security audits.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data

Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@soraengine.com
